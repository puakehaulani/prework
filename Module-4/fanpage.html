Daði Freyr


Bio

Daði Freyr Pétursson, known professionally as Daði Freyr or by the mononym Daði, is an Icelandic musician living in Berlin, Germany. He was due to represent Iceland in the Eurovision Song Contest 2020 with the song "Think About Things", before the Eurovision Song Contest 2020 was cancelled. He performs with a group of friends credited collectively as "Daði og Gagnamagnið".
